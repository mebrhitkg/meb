Certification Levels

  • International Referee: Certified by FIVB, eligible for continental and global competitions
  • National Referee: Certified by TVT, eligible for national championships
  • Regional Referee: Regional level certification for local and club competitions
  • Provisional Referee: Trainee-level certification for developing referees

Training & Development

Regular Certification Courses are held quarterly to develop new referees and upgrade existing certifications.

Workshops & Seminars: Monthly training sessions on new rules, positioning, and decision-making.

Mentorship Program: Pairing experienced referees with trainees for practical learning.
