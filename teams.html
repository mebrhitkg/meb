Club Profiles & Information

Mekelle Volleyball Club

📍 Home Base: Mekelle Sports Complex

Club Overview:

Mekelle Volleyball Club is one of the oldest and most prestigious clubs in the Tigray region. Founded in 2010, it has established itself as a powerhouse in regional volleyball competitions.

Teams:
  • Alpha Men's - League Leaders
  • Alpha Women's - Competitive Mid-Table
  • Youth Tigers - Youth Champions
Key Information:
  • Member Since: 2010
  • National Championships: 3 (2023, 2022, 2020)
  • Regional Cups: 5 titles
  • Home Venue: Mekelle Sports Complex (2,000 capacity)
  • Club President: Gebreselassie Abraha
Adigrat Volleyball Club

📍 Home Base: Adigrat Sports Arena
