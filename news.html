2024 National Championship Begins August 15

📅 July 7, 2024 | 👤 By TVT Communications

The Tigray Volleyball Federation proudly announces the commencement of the 2024 National Championship Season. This prestigious tournament will showcase the best volleyball talent from across the region with participation from over 40 registered clubs and teams.

Key Highlights:

  • Men's League Championship - 20 teams competing
  • Women's League Championship - 18 teams competing
  • Youth Championship - Mixed divisions
  • Location: Mekelle Sports Complex
  • Dates: August 15-30, 2024
