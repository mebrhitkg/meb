About Tigray Volleyball Federation

Promoting Excellence, Development, and Unity in Volleyball

History of TVT

The Tigray Volleyball Federation (TVT) was established in 1995 with a vision to develop and promote volleyball across the Tigray region. Since its inception, TVT has grown from a small grassroots organization to the leading authority for volleyball development in the region.

Key Milestones:
1995 - Foundation

TVT established as the regional governing body for volleyball with initial focus on youth development.

2000 - First National Championship

Organized the first regional championship with participation from 15 teams across all districts.

2008 - International Recognition

Became affiliated with the African Volleyball Confederation (CAVB) and participated in continental competitions.

2015 - Training Center Established

Opened the TVT Training Center with world-class facilities and coaching staff for athlete development.

2024 - Expansion & Growth

Current era marked by digital transformation, increased youth programs, and international partnerships.

Vision, Mission & Values

👁️
Vision

To be the premier volleyball federation in East Africa, recognized for excellence, innovation, and competitive achievement while fostering a culture of inclusive participation.

🎯
Mission

To develop volleyball at all levels, support and nurture talented athletes and coaches, organize competitive championships, and foster a culture of sport excellence and integrity.

Strategic Plan 2024-2028

Our comprehensive 5-year strategic plan outlines key priorities and goals for the federation's growth and development.

Strategic Pillars
1. Grassroots Development

Expand volleyball programs in all 8 districts with focus on youth talent identification and development.

2. Elite Athlete Support

Establish dedicated training centers for competitive athletes aiming for national and continental competition.

3. Coach & Referee Development

Strengthen certification programs and continuous professional development for coaching and officiating.

4. Infrastructure & Facilities

Upgrade and maintain world-class training facilities across the region.

5. International Participation

Increase representation in continental and international championships.

6. Sustainability & Funding

Secure diverse funding sources and build sustainable financial model for long-term operations.
